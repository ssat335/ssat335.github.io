Shameer Sathar, PhD

Hi, Welcome to my personal webpage.

I am currently working as a Data Scientist in the Health Intelligence Hub at the Northland Distric Health Board. I work closely with Dr Juliet Rumball-Smith (Director of Health Intelligence & Translational Medicine) and Mr John Wansbone (General Manager - Planning, Integration, People & Performance).

After completing my PhD in Bioengineering (Modelling and Computational Science), I joined University of Auckland as a full-time Research Fellow in the Auckland Bioengineering Institute. I currently have active collaboration with the Gastrointestinal System Research Group working lead by Professor Leo K. Cheng. My PhD thesis was focussed on developing novel computing techniques to model and predict the gastrointestional electrical activity.

Reseach Interests

My research interests are centered around image/signal processing using machine learning techniques and data-driven science. I specialise in semantic segmentation, image classification and recommender engines using deep neural networks. I am currently working on predictive models to identify high-risk patients and to develop models for describing patient pathways through the healthcare system.
